Sound of Metal - A Story of Deaf Identity

Transcript

"Sound of Metal (2019) follows Ruben, a heavy metal drummer who begins to lose his hearing. Forced to stay with a Deaf community, he confronts his identity and what silence truly means. The film challenges the assumption that deafness is something to be fixed instead portraying it as a culture and a way of being."
