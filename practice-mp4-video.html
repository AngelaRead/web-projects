Ship Bottom, NJ 2023

"Walters Homes, an award-winning custom design/build firm, is constructing a new office building to bring four of its divisions under one roof. The office facility will be located near the entrance and exit point to Long Beach Island on Central Ave in Ship Bottom.The two-story 5,790-square-foot building will house a Walters Homes sales office, Walters Realty, Walters Architecture LLC, and design center. This will be the second sales location for Walters Homes which maintains a sales office in Manahawkin and corporate offices in Barnegat."

Source: Donovan Architects
